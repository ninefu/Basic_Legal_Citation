§ 2-600. How to Cite Court Rules [BB|ALWD]

§ 2-600 Examples Window (restore)

Principle: Rules of evidence or procedure are cited by name of the set of rules (beginning with the jurisdiction) «e.g.» and the rule number «e.g.». The name is abbreviated. According to The Bluebook, the ALWD Guide to Legal Citation, and widespread practice, no date need be included so long as the citation's reference is to the rule currently in effect.
