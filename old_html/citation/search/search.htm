KEY WORD SEARCH
..[CLOSE SEARCH WINDOW]

Enter single words or phrases in the search window.

Two words separated by a space are treated as a phrase. For example, entering judicial opinion retrieves sections where those two words appear as a phrase but not those where the two words appear separately.

To search for sections where two or more words appear, but not necessarily as a phrase place a plus sign (and no space) between those words -- e.g., electronic+journal+url.
